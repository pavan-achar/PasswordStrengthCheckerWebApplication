How score is computed

We estimate entropy in bits using character pool size and length, penalize common passwords, repeated characters, simple sequences (e.g., "abcd", "1234"), and apply a small bonus for mixed character classes. Use the suggestions to increase strength.

Quick tips
  • Use length — add characters (12+ recommended).
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid common words, repeated sequences, and simple substitutions.
  • Use passphrases — 4+ unrelated words are strong & memorable.
